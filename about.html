About Ruang Kerja

Connecting professionals with Oppurtunities

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Ruang Kerja began as an initiative by a group of Informatics students who were deeply concerned about one of Indonesia’s biggest challenges — unemployment. We saw the gap between job seekers and opportunities, especially for fresh graduates and informal workers, and wanted to create a solution that could empower them through accessible technology.

Driven by this mission, we developed Ruang Kerja, a digital platform that helps connect job seekers with companies and institutions efficiently. By combining interactive design, user-friendly features, and an integrated approach, we aim to make job searching more informative, and impactful for everyone.
